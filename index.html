About Me

My name is Martin, and I make clean, responsive websites. I live with my girlfriend of 6 years in Bergen, Norway. My introduction to web-development first came when my sister (and shortly after, a colleague) asked me if I could make a website for them because they "figured it would be something you'd be good at". My interest was piqued, and after a little bit of practice with HTML and CSS, I was hooked.

I've since made it my mission to master the craft of front-end development. Although I'm still at the beginning of my web-developer journey, I take every day as a learning opportunity to become that little bit better. When I'm away from a computer and my work at a bakery, I spend my days listening to music and watching Liverpool matches. I go skiing in the winter, I go hiking in the summer, and I watch movies in between. Also, I love cats. Cats are cool (the movie, not so much).
